About Baobab Data Solutions

BDS Lda is a dynamic research and development firm specializing in data-driven solutions for sustainable development. Founded in Maputo, Mozambique, the company is comprised of a team of young, enthusiastic staff with experience in data collection using cutting-edge technology. Our team has managed large-scale and cross-sectional studies for multilateral donors and private sector entities in monitoring and impact evaluations.
